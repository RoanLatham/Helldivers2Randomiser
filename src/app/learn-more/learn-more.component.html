Helldivers 2 Loadout System

Weapons System

Helldivers 2 features a diverse arsenal of weapons categorized into three main types:

  • Primary Weapons: Your main firearm, ranging from assault rifles to shotguns and sniper rifles.

  • Secondary Weapons: Sidearms that complement your primary weapon or provide alternative fire options.

  • Throwable Weapons: Grenades and other throwable explosives for area damage and crowd control.

Each weapon category includes options for different combat scenarios, with unique attributes like fire rate, damage, range, and special effects. Our randomiser helps you discover unexpected combinations that might synergize brilliantly with your stratagems.

Stratagem System

Stratagems are special abilities called down from orbit that define your combat role in Helldivers 2. They're organized by ship modules:

  • Orbital Cannons: Devastating orbital strikes for area denial and massive damage.

  • Eagle Airstrikes: Versatile air support solutions for precision targeting and close air support, including rockets, bombs, and strafing runs.

  • Sentries: Automated defense turrets that assist in protecting objectives and controlling enemy approaches.

  • Backpacks: Utility items worn on your back that provide unique benefits.

  • Support Weapons: Heavy weapons that offer specialized firepower.

  • Defensive Measures: Shields, mines, and other protective systems.

  • Supply Options: Ammunition, reinforcements, and other consumables.

Choosing the right stratagems for your mission is crucial. Our randomiser helps you break from routine and try new combinations, potentially discovering more effective loadouts for different mission types.

Booster System

Boosters provide passive bonuses that enhance your Helldiver's capabilities. From increased armor to faster movement or quicker stratagem cooldowns, boosters can significantly impact your effectiveness on the battlefield.

The right booster can complement your weapon and stratagem choices, creating synergistic effects that elevate your combat performance. Our randomiser includes boosters in its selections, ensuring you experience all aspects of Helldivers 2's loadout system.

Warbond System

Warbonds are content packs in Helldivers 2 that contain themed collections of weapons, stratagems, and boosters. Each Warbond offers unique equipment with distinct visual styles and gameplay advantages.

Our randomiser allows filtering by Warbond, making it easy to customize your experience based on the content you own. This feature helps you quickly disable items from Warbonds you haven't purchased, ensuring your randomized loadouts only include equipment available to you.

Strategic Loadout Considerations

Mission-Specific Loadouts

Different mission types in Helldivers 2 require different approaches. Here are some considerations for major enemy types:

Terminid Tactics

When facing Terminids (bugs), consider loadouts with high area-of-effect damage to counter swarm tactics. Flamethrowers, rocket launchers, and incendiary stratagems excel against the bug menace.

Key Stratagem Types: Orbital Barrage, Incendiary Strike, Eagle Clusters

Automaton Approach

Automatons (bots) require precision and armor penetration. Anti-materiel rifles, railguns, and EMP weapons are effective against their armored units and electronic systems.

Key Stratagem Types: EMP Strike, Orbital Railcannon, Anti-Tank solutions

Our randomiser can help you discover unexpected combinations that work well against specific enemy types, expanding your tactical repertoire beyond conventional wisdom.
